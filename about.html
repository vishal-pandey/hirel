About us

Hirel is an HR technology startup that is revolutionizing the recruitment landscape. We are a product-based platform that addresses the challenges faced by two distinct types of companies:

Companies seeking top talent: Hirel provides a seamless solution by connecting them with HR consultancy firms. In today's competitive hiring landscape, relying on a few consultancies through contractual agreements limits the possibilities. Hirel eliminates the need for multiple contracts and complex negotiations by offering a platform where companies can register and post their job requirements. By leveraging the expertise of numerous HR consultancies, companies gain access to a vast pool of talent without the hassle of individual contracts.

HR consultancy firms: Hirel empowers HR consultancy firms by expanding their client base and driving more business opportunities. Traditionally, consultancies struggle to onboard new clients due to limited contractual agreements. Hirel breaks this barrier by acting as a mediator between consultancies and companies, eliminating the need for extensive documentation and negotiation. Consultancies can work with thousands of listed companies on our platform, seamlessly connecting and providing their services without the constraints of formal contracts. Hirel ensures transparency and efficiency throughout the hiring process. Both companies and consultancies establish a standardized contract with Hirel, simplifying the commercial aspects of the relationship. Hirel facilitates smooth communication, timely payments, and complete transparency between parties. Furthermore, consultancies can utilize Hirel as a product to share shortlisted resumes with their clients, ensuring privacy by hiding candidate contact details in the shared resumes.

An added advantage is that Hirel is completely free for both companies and HR consultancies. We believe in providing equal opportunities and fostering meaningful connections without financial barriers.

Experience the future of recruitment, where companies effortlessly find top talent, HR consultancies expand their client base, and all parties benefit from a free, innovative platform.
